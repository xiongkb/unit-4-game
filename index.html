Crystal Collector Game

Intro

There will be a number generated randomly. Your goal is to try and match that number with the crystals.


There are four crystals below. Each crystal is worth different points. Click on them to see how much they will add.


You win if you collect enough crystals to match the number. If you go over, then it's an automatic loss. When you lose, the number you have to match will change and you can try again with that number.


Keep in mind that when you lose or win, the the value of the crystals will change each time.
